This is the part where I get to tell you about myself and this website. My name is Tom Davidson and I put this website together for a school project for a ficticious company named CF Audio-Visual. I attempted to cover all requirements in the "Final Project Rubric" to get a successful grade.

For the general theme of my project I took a picture of a stereo speaker and blew it up to get the granual web look that you would only see close up. I then inserted to colors (red and blue) and took gradients of the colors along with some wind blown text to create my logo. After that I used variations of those colors as the theme to my website. All backgrounds, links, text and headers are differnt variants of the basic blue and red colors that I chose when creating my logo.


One of the requirements to this site was to include a video image. I have accomplished that on the Services page with a video of someone receiving a Samsung HD TV for Christmas.

Another of the requirements for this project was to have a form for this site that included a list. I have included such a form on the "Contact" page of this website. If it were a functioning form then the user would be able to enter what they looking for along with their contact information and I would then have info to help me secure a sale for my ficticious company.

Thank you for visiting my site. It has been fun but also a challenge to code a 5 page website without a GUI editor. Perhaps I will expand on these ideas further along in the SNHU program as I work towards by BA in IT and Telecommunications. If I had more time to work on this site I might have made some original videos to add showcasing entertainment systems and conference rooms that I currently have access to.
